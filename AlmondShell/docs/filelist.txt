`aengine.engine_components` instead of including the `aentity*.hpp`/
`acomponent*` headers directly. Platform and context consumers should prefer
`aengine.platform`, `aengine.context.*` partitions, and the backend partitions
(`acontext.opengl.*`, `acontext.sdl.*`, `acontext.raylib.*`,
`acontext.softrenderer.*`).
